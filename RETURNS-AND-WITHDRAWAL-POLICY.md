# Flowva — Returns & Withdrawal Policy

> **DRAFT — review by a Dutch/EU consumer-law adviser before go-live.**
> Written to give Flowva the strongest position the law allows: the customer
> bears the return cost and responsibility, refunds are product-only and paid
> after inspection, and our QC photos defend against false "defective" claims.
> A consumer's 14-day right of withdrawal for standard goods **cannot** be
> removed — this policy works *within* that right, not against it.

_Last updated: [date] · Seller: Flowva ([legal name / KvK], the Netherlands)_

---

## 1. Who you buy from
You purchase from **Flowva**. Flowva curates the products, sets the price, adds
a service fee, and arranges sourcing, quality control and international shipping
on your behalf. Flowva is the seller for the purposes of EU consumer law.

## 2. Your 14-day right of withdrawal
As a consumer in the EU you may withdraw from your purchase **within 14 days
without giving a reason.** The 14-day period starts on the day **you (or someone
you nominate) receive the item** — not on the day you order. Because our items
ship from China, your 14 days begin **when the parcel arrives**, so the shipping
time never shortens your window. You may also withdraw at any time **before** the
item arrives.

## 3. How to withdraw
Use the **"Withdraw / cancel order"** button in the app (or email
[support@flowva.app]). No account login is required. Provide your **name + order
number + email**. You will receive an **automatic confirmation** of your request.
You then have 14 days from that request to send the item back.

## 4. What you get refunded
- **Partial return (you keep some items):** we refund the **product price of the
  returned item(s) only.** We do **not** refund shipping for a partial return —
  shipping is a separate, already-performed service that delivered your other
  items.
- **Full withdrawal (whole order):** we refund the product price plus the
  **standard** outbound delivery cost (any premium/express upgrade you chose is
  not refunded).
- **Service fee:** covers the sourcing, purchasing and quality-control work that
  is already carried out for your order. [Adviser to confirm refundability on
  full withdrawal under NL law.]

## 5. Return shipping costs — paid by you
You pay the cost of returning the item. We provide a **return address in the
Netherlands**, so returning is cheap and local — you do **not** send anything
back to China. Send returns to:

> Flowva Returns — [NL return address]

Items must be returned within 14 days of your withdrawal request.

## 6. Condition of returned items
Return items **in their original condition** (unworn/unused beyond what is needed
to inspect them, with tags and packaging). You are liable for any **diminished
value** caused by handling beyond what is necessary to establish the nature and
characteristics of the item — we may **deduct** that diminished value from your
refund. Items returned damaged, worn or incomplete may receive a **reduced
refund** or, where appropriate, no refund.

## 7. Refund method and timing
We refund within **14 days** of receiving the returned item back (or of you
proving you have sent it, whichever is earlier). Refunds are credited to your
**Flowva balance** by default. [Adviser to confirm whether NL law requires refund
to the original card; if so, we offer the original payment method on request.]

## 8. Cancelling during fulfilment
Because items ship from China, there is a window before your item is shipped:
- **Before we have purchased the item** — we cancel at no cost and refund you in
  full.
- **After it has shipped to you** — it is handled as a return under this policy
  (sections 3–7): the parcel is delivered, you return it to our NL address.

## 9. Items excluded from the right of withdrawal
The 14-day right does **not** apply to, among others:
- **Custom-made or personalised products** (made to your specification);
- **Sealed hygiene/health items** once the seal is broken;
- **Perishable goods**;
- other categories excluded by law.
These exclusions are stated on the product page where relevant.

## 10. Faulty, damaged or wrong items (separate from withdrawal)
If an item arrives **defective, damaged in transit, or not as described**, this is
**not** a withdrawal — it is covered by your statutory warranty. In that case **we
cover the return cost** and provide a **repair, replacement or full refund**.
- Every item is **quality-controlled and photographed** before it ships. These QC
  photos record the item's condition at dispatch and are used to assess any claim.
- Minor variations from the supplier's marketing photos (slight colour/texture
  differences) are normal for factory-direct goods and are not a defect.
- Report a problem through the app within a reasonable time after delivery, with
  photos.

## 11. General
- Prices shown are the real factory price; a separate service fee and
  international shipping apply and are shown before you pay.
- This policy does not affect your mandatory statutory rights as a consumer.
- Governing law: the Netherlands.
