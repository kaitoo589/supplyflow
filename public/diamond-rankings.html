How Do 1688 Factory Diamond Rankings Work?

1688 uses a factory ranking system to evaluate supplier performance. The more diamonds a factory has, the stronger its recent performance is considered by 1688.

The ranking is based on performance data from the past 30–90 days, including:

  • On-time delivery performance
  • Customer service responsiveness
  • Custom manufacturing transaction volume
  • Customer satisfaction
  • Repeat purchase rate

Ranking Levels

No diamond

A new or less active factory.

The supplier may not yet have enough transaction history or performance data to qualify for a diamond ranking.

💎
1 Diamond

A supplier with solid baseline performance.

The factory meets 1688's minimum standards for service quality, delivery reliability, and transaction activity.

💎💎
2 Diamonds

Above-average performance.

The factory demonstrates stronger reliability, customer service, and order volume compared to lower-ranked suppliers.

💎💎💎
3 Diamonds

A high-performing supplier.

The factory consistently achieves strong results in delivery, communication, and customer satisfaction.

💎💎💎💎
4 Diamonds

The highest level within the diamond ranking system.

These suppliers maintain excellent operational performance and a proven track record across all major performance indicators.

How Does 1688 Calculate the Ranking?

According to 1688's official indicator definitions, supplier rankings are based on several key metrics:

IndicatorDescription
On-Time Delivery RatePercentage of orders delivered within the agreed timeframe
Service Response RatePercentage of inquiries that receive a timely response from the supplier
Custom Transaction ScoreValue of custom manufacturing orders completed through the platform
Repurchase RatePercentage of customers who place repeat orders
Interested Customer CountNumber of customers who have engaged in serious negotiations with the supplier

As these performance indicators improve, a supplier's ranking can increase.

Source

This explanation is based on the official 1688 supplier performance definitions (“指标定义”) displayed within supplier profiles on the 1688 platform, including metrics such as On-Time Delivery Rate, Service Response Rate, Repurchase Rate, Custom Manufacturing Transactions, and Interested Customer Count.
